Batman is a fictional superhero who appears in published by DC Comics. The character was created by Bob Kane and Bill Finger, 
and first appeared in Detective Comics #27 (March 1939). Batman is the secret identity of Bruce Wayne, a wealthy American playboy, 
philanthropist, and industrialist who operates as a vigilante in Gotham City, battling a variety of criminals and villains.

Superman is a fictional superhero who appears in published by DC Comics. The character was created by Jerry Siegel and Joe Shuster, 
and first appeared in Action Comics #1 (June 1938). Superman is the alter ego of Kal-El, an alien infant from the planet Krypton who
was sent to Earth as an infant by his parents, Jor-El and Lara, moments before the destruction of his home planet. He was raised by 
Jonathan and Martha Kent in the small town of Smallville, Kansas, where he learned to control his superhuman abilities.

Wonder Woman is a fictional superheroine who appears in published by DC Comics. The character was created by William Moulton Marston 
and H. G. Peter, and first appeared in All-American Comics #8 (December 1941). Wonder Woman is the Amazonian princess Diana of 
Themyscira, who was blessed by the Greek gods with superhuman powers. She left her home island to fight in World War II and defend 
humanity from threats.
